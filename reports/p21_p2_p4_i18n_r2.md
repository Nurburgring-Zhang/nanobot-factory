## Key details

### 1. Task spec's "9 locales" — actually 10

The task spec said "all 9 locales" (en, zh-CN, ja, ko, es, fr, de, pt, ar). The project actually has **10 locales**: the 9 listed + **ru-RU** (Russian, the original 9th locale, pre-dating pt-PT). P2 P2 added **pt-PT** (Portuguese) as the 10th locale to literally satisfy the "pt.ts" requirement. Round 2 keeps both ru-RU and pt-PT — we add 100 keys to all 10 locales, not just 9.

### 2. Unnamespaced keys at the TOP level (vs. under `common`)

vue-i18n resolves `t('Cancel')` (no dot) by looking up `Cancel` at the TOP level of the messages object. The first attempt mistakenly put the unnamespaced keys under the `common` namespace, which means `t('Cancel')` would NOT find them and the global command palette / search components (`ShortcutHelp.vue`, `QuickNav.vue`, `GlobalSearch.vue`, `DragUpload.vue`) would have used their hardcoded fallback (`'关闭'`, `'展开'`, etc.) instead of the translated value. The final fix places them at the top level. The new test `i18n_keys_round2.test.ts` has a dedicated test for this.

Source-level usage of the unnamespaced keys:
- `ShortcutHelp.vue:75` — `t('Close', '关闭')` — for the Escape-key shortcut help
- `QuickNav.vue:185,187,188,196` — `t('Expand', '展开')`, `t('Favorites', '收藏')`, `t('Recent', '最近')`, `t('Clear', '清除')`
- `GlobalSearch.vue:216,223,224` — `t('Close', '关闭')`, `t('Navigate', '选择')`, `t('Open', '打开')`
- `DragUpload.vue:269,270,271` — `t('Retry', '重试')`, `t('Cancel', '取消')`, `t('Remove', '移除')`

After round 2, all 10 components will use the translated value (not the hardcoded fallback) for the listed keys.
